LEMON-GARLIC CHICKEN KEBABS

DISCRIPTION

When we talk about juicy;tender;scrampcious and mouth watering nothing beatslemon garlic chicken kebabs This easy to prepare dish does not need much just a quest for great delicious snack on the go.Lets go see if we can make us great chicken.

INGRIDIENTS

  • 1/4 cup butter,melted
  • 1 lemon,juiced and zested
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar
  • 1 clove garlic,minced or pressed
  • 4 boneless chicken breasts,cubed
  • 4 skewers,or as needed

DIRECTIONS

  1. Combine butter,lemon juice,zest,brown sugar,and garlic
  2. Add chicken and marinate for at least an hour
  3. Preheat grill for high heat
  4. Remove chicken from the marinade and shake off exess then thread onto skewers
  5. Lightly oil the grill grate.Arrange skewers on the prepared grill.Cook 15 to 20 minutes,turning occassionally until chicken juices run clear
