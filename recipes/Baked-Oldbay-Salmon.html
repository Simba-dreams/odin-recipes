BAKED OLDBAY SALMON

DESCRIPTION

This is the easiest way of making a baked salmon in the comfort of your home and enjoy a tender and succulentsalmon. This recipe you will use mostly things that we regularly find in our kitchens.

INGRIDIENTS

  • 1 serving cooking spray
  • 2 pounds salmon fillet
  • 2 tablespoons mayonnaise
  • 2 tablespoons Oldbay seasoning
  1. Pre heat the oven to 350 degrees F
  2. Line a large baking sheet with aluminium foil and spray with cooking spray
  3. Place salmon on the prepared baking sheet spread with mayonnaise and sprinkle with Oldbay
  4. Bake in preheated oven until fish flakes easily with a fork about 20 min
