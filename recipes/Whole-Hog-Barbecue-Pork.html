Whole-Hog-Barbecue-Pork

DESCRIPTION

Not everytime do we find ourselves with a 6 foot barbecue pit.Or with a full hog itself So this recipe will show you how you can enjoy the best of a whole carolina-style -hog-barbecue-pork. By using the instructions and following them step by step.You will be able to enjoy yourself a whole roast barbecue without the hustles of a pit

INGREDIENTS

  • 2.5 pounds baby back pork ribs
  • 1.5 pounds pork shoulder roast
  • 1.5 pounds pork tenderloin,trimmed
  • 1 pound porkbelly
  • 0.5 pounds sirloin roast

FOR THE RUB

  • 5 teaspoons kosher salt
  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 2 teaspoons smoked salt
  • 2 teaspoons smoked paprika
  • 2 teaspoons ground black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon ground cummin
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 0.5 teaspoon cayenne pepper

STEPS

  1. Pre heat the oven to 250 degrees
  2. Cut all the meat portions in half
  3. Combine all the rubbing ingredients in a small bowl
  4. Place a piece of heav-yduty foil on a sheet pan.Half ribs down in the centre of the foil
  5. Top up with all the other cuts save for the other rib half that will cover them
  6. Wrap tightly in 3 more layers of foil
  7. Transfer to the oven
  8. Roast in the preheated oven until the meat is falling off the bone and very very tender for 9 hours
  9. Remove bones and pull/shred meat before serving
